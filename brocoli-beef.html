Brocoli beef

Description

Broccoli beef is nutritious and low in sugar and fat. An amazing choice for people on a diet

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. beef sliced, put salt, soy sauce, pepper, cornstarch, a little cooking wine, a little shredded ginger, mix well and then add some peanut oil and mix down
  2. broccoli washed and cut into small pieces
  3. pan hot, put oil (better put more), burst ginger, put the beef into the pan and stir fry
  4. put oil to burn garlic, put the broccoli and carrot slices into the same stir-fry cooked, and then the fried beef also poured into the pan together
